El Pollo Loco

Introduction

Welcome to El Pollo Loco!

The target is to kill the endboss a big angry chicken.
But it's not so easy to come there because you have to avoid contact with the other chickens otherwise you get hurt and lose livepoints.

There are salsa bottles and coins scattered all over the map.

You can collect maximum five salsa bottles and coins. If you've collected five coins a new bottle spawns on a random place into the map. This is possible two times there are ten coins totally to collect. You can always check your statusbar for showing how many life points your character or the endboss has left.

You also see how many salsa bottles or coins you've already collected. The status bar from the coins starts to count at zero after collecting five.

If you win or lose a game you have the chance to start the game new or return to the start screen.

At the bottom you see another button to show the legend which showing you how to controll the game.

Click the button to show the legend and to close click into the text.

⬅ = move left
➞ = move right
— = jump
D = throw bottle
P = Fullscreen esc = leave Fullscreen
